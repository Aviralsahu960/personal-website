Building Vyntax: From Idea to Reality

By Aviral | Dec 12, 2025

When I started learning frontend development, I realized that watching tutorials wasn't enough. I needed to build something real. That's how Vyntax was born.

Why Vanilla JS?

In a world obsessed with React, I chose to build Vyntax using pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Why? Because I wanted to understand the fundamentals.

The "Dark Mode" Aesthetic

I wanted the site to feel like a hacker's terminal but modern. I used a deep palette (`#020617`) mixed with neon accents like Peacock Green and Electric Purple.

What's Next?

Vyntax is just the beginning. I plan to add more tools, including a Regex tester and a Flexbox playground. Stay tuned!
