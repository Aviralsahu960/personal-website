Student Life

Balancing Code & College at VIT Bhopal

By Aviral | Dec 05, 2025

Life at VIT Bhopal is a unique experience. Between the futuristic campus architecture and the rigorous CS curriculum, finding time to code for yourself can be tough. But that's where the real growth happens.

The Night Shift

Most of Vyntax was built after 10 PM. There's something peaceful about coding when the hostel is quiet. It's just you, VS Code, and a few bugs to fix.

From Kanpur to Bhopal

Coming from Kanpur, UP, moving to Bhopal was a big shift. The calm environment of the campus is perfect for focusing on tech. Being surrounded by other ambitious tech enthusiasts at VIT pushes me to be better every day.

If you are a student reading this: Don't just rely on your syllabus. Build side projects. Break things. That's how you actually learn.
